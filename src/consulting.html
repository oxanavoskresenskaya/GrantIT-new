Software update makes the presented program more stable and secure. It can also improve the efficiency of the program as well as fix previously unknown bugs. After updating the software, the program can provide more convenience and functionality for users. Software updates are carried out by our experienced specialists who have knowledge and experience in programming and other IT-related areas.     Software audit

    Software audit is the process of checking, researching and studying software to evaluate its functionality, quality, safety and compatibility. The purpose of an audit is to verify that the software meets the stated requirements. This helps to detect errors and problems that may arise during the development and operation of the software. Our specialists use a special software auditor that performs various types of analysis, such as structural, functional, performance, interface, security, etc. Various tools are used to conduct an audit, such as automation tools, security scanners, a static code analyzer, etc. These tools help you detect bugs, problems, and hazards that may occur during the development and operation of software. Software audit is an important step in the software development and maintenance process. It allows you to detect and correct problems before they appear in the process of work. Software audit helps to improve the performance and efficiency of the software and improve the quality of the product.
