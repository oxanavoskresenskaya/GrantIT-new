    Technical support when using the software

    Technical support provides the software customer with assistance and guidance on how to use the software, as well as solving problems that may arise when using the software. This can be both partial training in the use of the software, and the provision of effective technical support to administrators and users. Also included in the technical support of the software is the process of updating and fixing errors in the software.

    Providing warranty and post-warranty software repair

    Warranty repairs include free correction of faults that occurred during the warranty period. The customer has the right to declare a breach of warranty obligations within the period specified in the EULA (End User License Agreement) from the date of purchase of the software. Post-warranty repair involves the paid correction of malfunctions that occurred after the expiration of the warranty period. In this case, the customer is not entitled to claim a breach of warranty. In both cases, the software repair process involves diagnosing the problem, analyzing the causes, and applying appropriate solutions. In some cases, when fixing problems, it may be necessary to replace components or even the entire software. When providing warranty and post-warranty services for software repair, our specialists provide high-quality diagnostics, restoration of software functionality, checking the functionality and correctness of the work.
