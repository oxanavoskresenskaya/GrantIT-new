    Technical support for the use of the software

    Technical support involves providing the software customer with help and guidance on how to use the software, as well as solving problems that may arise when using the software. This can be as partial training on how to use the software as well as providing administrators and users with effective technical support. Also included in software support is the process of updating and correcting software bugs.

    Providing warranty and post-warranty repair of the software

    Warranty repair implies free of charge repair of faults which occurred during the warranty period. The Customer shall have the right to claim a breach of warranty within the period specified in the EULA (End User License Agreement) from the date of the Software purchase. The post-warranty repair includes paid repair of defects which occurred after the expiry of the free warranty period.. In this case, the customer has no right to claim a breach of warranty. In both cases, the software repair process involves fault diagnosis, analyzing the causes, and applying appropriate solutions. In some cases, the repair process may require the replacement of components or even the entire software.
